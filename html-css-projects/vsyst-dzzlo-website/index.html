Dzzlo-OMS

Dzzlo OMS app is an order management system for ordering fuel. There will be details of transactions between customer and dealer starting from ordering, making, invoice, entering payment details, and can view all of these.

about the app

how it works?

Dealers

Displays all the Dealers with their respective details and account.

Users

Displays varies users of the company at different levels with specific accessibility.

Transactions

Displays all the transactions between the dealer and customer such as orders, account.

Vehicles

Displays all the vehicles of the company along with drivers alloted.
